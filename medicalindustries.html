Tribals and their response to covid:

Tribe is a very sensitive community and is needed to be taken care of. There is significant population of tribes in India.
Lack of basic healthcare infrastructure, precarious birth practices, poor nutrition and unsafe water are some of the features of rural India. As a significant chunk of the tribal population belongs to rural areas, it is expected that healthcare facilities for them are not as robust compared to facilities availed by other communities. Some communities are:
1. Gonds Tribes
2. Bhils Tribes
3. Santhal Tribes
4. Great Andamanese Tribes
5. Khasi Tribes
6. Garo Tribes
7. Angami Tribes
8. Munda Tribes

Any firm along with collaboration of other firm can apply for the vaccination camps in these localities. Such firms are provided with more incentives by government.

There is no doubt over the fact that tribes live in the vicinity of forests. There are wide range of medicies that require raw materials that are produced in the forests. These medicinal plants are not easy to find in the dense forest. Tribal community has a vast knowledge over this.
The firm that helps in the vaccination of tribal community has the permissible authority to harness these resources from the forest and even can take the help of tribal communities.
