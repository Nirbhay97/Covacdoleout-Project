How remaining industries can nurture the plant of clemency:

The vaccines has to travel a long way to reach the target audience

There has a number of cases where the vaccines were wasted because of no proper cold storages and less number of people reporting for the vaccination than the expected ones.

If any firm wants to establish cold storage for the vaccines, it would be given corresponding benefits in near future.

The firms can apply for the cold storage through the app covacdoleout.
There is also a dire need for the portable vaccine centers to be made. The firm has the flexibilty to choose between the portable vaccine centers and fixed ones as per suitability. It can register at the CoVacdoleout app.
